We craft things that people use and love ...

You have always thinking of games as one source of violence, crimes and laziness? Of course you are right as it is not difficult to name some of the many cases in which games are to blame as having bad influence on players.

Amid the negative public perception, one team in Vietnam has created a game- “Soft Wings”

When asked why he named it “soft wings”, its creator - Duong Nguyen Hung said in our meeting that this game is inspired by the continuing tension in the East Sea as well as in other parts of the World.

“We have been experiencing unprecedented changes around the world with crises, wars, conflicts, diseases and other threats to the peace, stability and prosperity of humankind. From the marching in Habib Bourguiba Avenue in Tunis, the waves of instability were spreading at unimaginable pace to Yemen, Egypt, Bahrain, Syria and Libya, and it is now ongoing concern to other parts of the globe.”

He added: “That is not to mention the Ukrainian conflict with the involvement of almost all major powers. Even in the Far East where people have a long-tradition of hard-working and peace-loving like China, Japan, Koreas and other South East Asian countries”

We wonder if the people around the world have let peace and stability out of their arm-spread just by negligence.

As game developers, his team would like to send a message to the world, the game is simple yet challenging as it requires players to control a paper plane flying through war vehicles in the sea with different settings.

No fire, no killing, no violence- but still you will feel the vulnerability in your hands.
“That’s all we want you to feel!”

Enjoy the game!
