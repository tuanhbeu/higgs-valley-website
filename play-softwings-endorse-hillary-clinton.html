Play Soft Wings. Endorse Hillary Clinton

Tap and tilt to fly your paper airplane over a sea of battleships and choppers.

Soft Wings advocates international engagement in the maintenance of peace and security in Asia-Pacific.

In 2011, the then Madam Secretary Hillary Clinton introduced the rebalance policy towards Asia, marking the U.S.’ first step to greater commitment to international affairs. Soft Wings therefore proudly supports Hillary Clinton’s presidential campaign and hopes she can bring progress to the rebalance agenda and give a hand to facilitate peace building in the Asia-Pacific region.

“Balancing and Rebalancing - that’s the mantra of this game!”

- Hung Duong
