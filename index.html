Soft Wings Mobile Game

Everyone needs a champion

Tap and tilt to fly your paper airplane over a sea of battleships and choppers. Soft Wings advocates international engagement in the maintenance of peace and security in Asia-Pacific.

In 2011, the then Madam Secretary Hillary Clinton introduced the rebalance policy towards Asia, marking the U.S.’ first step to greater commitment to international affairs. Soft Wings therefore proudly supports Hillary Clinton’s presidential campaign and hopes she can bring progress to the rebalance agenda and give a hand to facilitate peace building in the Asia-Pacific region.

Everyday Americans need a champion.
I want to be that champion.

Balancing and rebalancing. That’s the mantra of the game.

Features:
- World Ranking for Players
- Result Sharing on Facebook
